**DocSaathi– AI Legal & Medical Document Simplifier**

DocSaathi is an AI-powered web app that converts complex legal and medical documents into simple, human-readable summaries.
The goal is simple: make difficult documents understandable for everyone.

Whether it’s a hospital report, prescription, insurance paper, agreement, or legal notice, DocSaathi extracts the important information and explains it in plain language.

Problem

Most people struggle to understand:

Medical reports
Prescriptions
Insurance papers
Legal agreements
Government documents

These documents are often filled with technical terms and confusing language.

DocSaathi solves this by using AI to generate:

Simple summaries
Key insights
Important warnings
Actionable points
